Why Ginkgo tree ?

The Ginkgo tree, known as a "living fossil," survived the atomic bombing of Hiroshima in 1945. Despite widespread destruction, several Ginkgo trees near the blast site budded and regrew, symbolizing resilience and hope. This remarkable survival has made the Ginkgo leaf a global emblem of endurance and renewal.

Why EarthBased Chose the Ginkgo Leaf

EarthBased selected the Ginkgo leaf as its logo element to symbolize resilience, growth, and renewal, aligning with their mission to foster a sustainable, plant-based lifestyle through a supportive, community-driven platform.
