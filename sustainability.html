Partnering with Grow Trees

One of our most significant collaborations is with Grow Trees, a pioneering organization dedicated to the global planting of trees. Trusted by over 700 multinational corporations, government agencies, PSUs, and private companies—including Blue Dart-DHL, TATA AIA, Reserve Bank of India, FICCI, NSE, and L&T Infotech—Grow Trees has made remarkable strides in environmental sustainability.

To date, Grow Trees has planted over 11 million trees across 23 states on public lands, creating more than 919,000 workdays for rural and tribal communities. These trees are projected to absorb over 222 million kgs of CO2 per year once mature.

At EarthBased, we proudly contribute to this impactful initiative by supporting the planting of 175 trees. Our contributions are enhancing the lifestyle and ecosystem of various wildlife species, including Tigers, Leopards, and the Hangul.

Building a Circular Economy

To address the pressing issue of waste, we've forged strategic partnerships with pioneering waste management organizations. By collaborating with ScrapQ, Crap Bin, Bintix, Vital Waste, and Saahas, we're actively contributing to a circular economy. These remarkable companies are at the forefront of innovative waste management solutions, transforming discarded materials into valuable resources.
